# اَلْقِسْمُ الْأَوَّلُ الْأَفْعَالُ النَّاقِصَةُ 

The first قسم of أفعال السماعي is الفعل الناقص.

## وَهِيَ أَفْعَالٌ تَدْخُلُ عَلَى الْمُبْتَدَأِ وَ الْخَبَرِ 

> تدخل means "to enter upon", and will be translated as "preceeds"

The أفعال الناقصة are أفعال which preceed the مبتدأ والخبر. The أفعال الناقصة enter before a جملة اسمية.

## وَ تَرْفَعُ الْأَوَّلَ وَ يُسَمَّى اسْمَهَا 

> The word سمى in arabic means "to name". For example, in the قرآن it comes, "وَإِنِّي سَمَّيْتُهَا مَرْيَمَ". Here, سمى is a فعل, and تُ is the فاعل, the مفعول به is "ها", and مريم is the مفعول به ثان (secondary object). When the فعل becomes مجهول, the مفعول به becomes the ناإب الفاعل and the مفعول به ثان becomes the مفعول به.

It gives رفع to the first, and it is called the اسم of the فعل الناقص.  Meaning, it causes the مبتدأ to be مرفوع and the مبتدأ is named the اسم of that فعل الناقص.


## وَ تَنْصِبُ الثَّانِيَ وَ يُسَمَّى خَبَرَهَا

And it causes the خبر to be منصوب, and names it as خبر of the فعل الناقص preceeding it, such as خبر كان or خبر ليس rather than just خبر.

:::note 

## (وَ قَدْ يَكُوْنُ خَبَرُهَا جُمْلَةً)

Sometimes, the خبر of the فعل الناقص may be a جملة.

:::

## وَ هِيَ أَرْبعَةُ أَضْرُبٍ 

And they are composed of four subcategories:
- كان وأخواتها (كان and أفعال related to كان)
- أَفْعَالُ الرَّجَاءِ (أفعال showing hope)
- أَفْعَالُ الْمُقَارَبَةِ (أفعال which show that the فعل is close to occurring)
- أَفْعَالُ الشُّرُوْعِ (أفعال which show that the action has taken place)

### الضَّرْبُ الْأَوَّلُ كَانَ وَأَخَوَاتُهَا، وَهِيَ ثَلَاثَةَ عَشَرَ

The first type of أفعال السماعي are أفعال الناقصة, and the first type of أفعال الناقصة are كان وأخواتها. And there are thirteen of them. Many of the أفعال الناقصة show some form of tense or give a timeframe to the اسم and خبر.

#### الْأَوَّلُ كَانَ، نَحْوُ كَانَ الرَّجُلُ مَرِيْضًا

The first is كان. The word كان means "was" in english. For example, "كان الرجل مريضا" means "the man was sick".

كان enters on to a مبتدأ  and خبر, the مبتدأ's name changes to اسم كان and it stays مرفوع, and the خبر (مريضا) is called خبر كان and becomes منصوب.

#### الثَّانِيْ أَصْبَحَ، نَحْوُ أَصْبَحَ الرَّجُلُ بَارِئًا

The second is أصبح. In arabic, صبح means "morning". What this فعل ناقص does is that it puts a timeframe on the مبتدأ and the خبر. 

So the example, "أَصْبَحَ الرَّجُلُ بَارِئًا" translates to "The man became  healthy in the morning". Perhaps, the man was sick the night before and he became healthy in the morning: كان الرجل مريضا (the man was sick), أَصْبَحَ الرَّجُلُ بَارِئًا (the man was healthy in the morning). 

:::tip

كان denotes that the اسم كان was the خبر كان any time in the past, whereas أصبح means that it was in the morning. If it is فعل ماض (in the past tense), it will mean the previous morning, and if it is يُصبح, it would mean the next morning.

::: 

#### الثَّالِثُ أَضْحَی، نَحْوُ: أَضْحَی الرَّجُلُ عَامِلًا


The third is أضحى. The word ضحى means "forenoon" in english. It also puts a timeframe on the اسم أضحى and اسم أضحى, but this time it means that it was prior to noon. 

For example, أَضْحَی الرَّجُلُ عَامِلًا mean that the man spent the forenoon working. 


#### الرَّابِعُ أَمْسَی، نَحْوُ أَمْسَی الرَّجُلُ كَالًّا

The fourth is أمسى. In arabic, مسى means evening. It puts the timeframe that the اسم أمسى was خبر أمسى in the evening.

In the example, أَمْسَی الرَّجُلُ كَالًّا means the man was tired in the evening. He was working in the forenoon and he was tired in the evening.

#### الْخَامِسُ ظَلَّ، نَحْوُ ظَلَّ الصَّائِمُ مُسْتَغْفِرًا

The fifth is ظل, and it means "all day long", and it denotes that the اس ظلم  was the خبر ظل all day long. 

:::info[FUN FACT]

In arabic, "ظِلٌّ" means shadow, and shadows can exist only during the day. This is why the verb ظل means "during the day.."

:::

In the example, ظل الصائم مستغفرا means that the fasting person spent the day seeking forgiveness.

#### السَّادِسُ بَاتَ، نَحْوُ بَاتَ الْعَابِدُ سَاجِدًا

The sixth فعل الناقص is بات, and it means during the night.

:::info[FUN FACT]

In arabic, بيت means house. Normally, people sleep at night and go out to work in the morning. They come home at night and spend the night in their بيت. Meaning, بيت is a house where you spend the night. بات, and يبيت means to spend the night.

:::

For example, بَاتَ الْعَابِدُ سَاجِدًا means that the worshipper spent the night in prostration.

#### السَّابِعُ صَارَ، نَحْوُ صَارَ الْجَاهِلُ عَالِمًا

The seventh is صار, and it means "to become". To change from one state to another. 

For example, صَارَ الْجَاهِلُ عَالِمًا means the ignorant person **became** learned.

:::note

صار does not put a timeframe on its اسم or خبر. It just means that the اسم became the خبر.

:::

#### الثَّامِنُ مَا بَرِحَ، نَحْوُ مَا بَرِحَ الْحَاجُّ مُلَبِّيًا

:::tip

The next few أفعال الناقصة with ما before them all mean "to continue to", but give different connotations.

:::

The eigth is مَا بَرِحَ. It means "to continue to". برح means to "stop", and the full verb ما برح with a نفي before it means that they did not stop (i.e., they continued).

For example, مَا بَرِحَ الْحَاجُّ مُلَبِّيًا means that the pilgrims continued to recite the تلبية (i.e., they did not stop reciting it).

#### التَّاسِعُ مَا انْفَكَّ، نَحْوُ: مَا انْفَكَّ الْمُذْنِبُ رَاجِيًا

The ninth is ما انفك, and it gives the same denotation as ما برح. It means to continue.

For example, مَا انْفَكَّ الْمُذْنِبُ رَاجِيًا means the sinful person remained hopeful.

:::info[RECAP]

The sentence المذنب راجيا means the wrongdoer is hopeful, and ما انفك المذنب راجيا means the wrongdoer remained hopeful. 

:::

#### الْعَاشِرُ مَا زَالَ، نَحْوُ مَا زَالَ الْحُجَّاجُ وَاقِفِيْنَ

The tenth is ما زال, and it also means to continue.

For example, مَا زَالَ الْحُجَّاجُ وَاقِفِيْنَ means that the pilgrims remained standing.

#### الْحَادِي عَشَرَ مَا فَتِئَ، نَحْوُ مَا فَتِئَ الْمُذْنِبُ نَادِمًا

The eleventh is ما فتئ, and it means to continue.

For example, مَا فَتِئَ الْمُذْنِبُ نَادِمًا means that the sinful person remained remorseful/regretful.

#### الثَّانِيْ عَشَرَ مَا دَامَ، نَحْوُ: أَطْلُبُ الْعِلْمَ مَا دُمْتُ حَيًّا

The twelfth is ما دام, and it is slightly different. It means "as long as". 

For example,  أَطْلُبُ الْعِلْمَ مَا دُمْتُ حَيًّا means "I will seek knowledge for as long as I live".

:::note

The ضمير (pronoun) تُ is the اسم ما دام and حَيًّا is the خبر.

:::

#### الثَّالِثُ عَشَرَ: لَيْسَ، نَحْوُ لَيْسَ رَبُّنَا ظَالِمًا

The last فعل of كان وأخواتها is ليس. And it is a نفي (negating particle).

For example, لَيْسَ رَبُّنَا ظَالِمًا means our lord is not an oppressor.

We have now completed كان وأخواتها.

Now, we shall move on to the أَفْعَالُ الرَّجَاءِ.

### الضَّرْبُ الثَّانِيْ أَفْعَالُ الرَّجَاءِ

The second type of فعل ناقص are the أفعال مقاربة, and they are أفعال which show hope.

:::info

In arabic, رجى/يرجو means to hope. Meaning, the أفعال رجاء show that the متكلم wants the اسم and خبر to link together.

:::

#### نَحْوُ عَسَى اللَّهُ أَن يَقْبَلَ صَوْمَ الْمُسْلِمِيْنَ 

For example, لفظ الجلالة (the word الله) becomes the اسم عسى, and أن وما بعدها is the خبر.. And يقبل سوم المسلمين means that الله accepts the fast of the believer. This connotes that the speaker hopes that الله accepts the fast of the believer.

عسى means hopefully.

:::tip

Usually when using عسى, you have أن on the خبر.

:::

### اَلضَّرْبُ الثَّالِثُ أَفْعَالُ الْمُقَارَبَةِ

The third type of أفعال الناقصة are the  أفعال المقاربة, which show proximity of the اسم to the خبر.

#### نَحْوُ كَادَ الْمُؤَذِّنُ يُؤَذِّنُ

For example, we have a مبتدأ (المؤذن) which is named خبر كاد, and the خبر (يُؤَذِّنُ)is a جملة. This by itself means the مؤذن is giving أذان. However, the preceeding فعل المقاربة gives the meaning that the مؤذن is about to give أذان (i.e., he is not giving it right now, but he will give it very soon). It means that the link between the مبتدأ and خبر is close to taking place.



:::info

In arabic, قريب means "close" (i.e., not far away). These أفعال show مقاربة (closeness). It means that the فعل is close to occurring.

:::



### الضَّرْبُ الرَّابِعُ أَفْعَالُ الشُّرُوْعِ

The fourth type of فعل ناقص are the أفعال الشروع, and these أفعال show that the اسم and خبر have started being linked (i.e., verbs of commencement).

#### نَحْوُ طَفِقَ الصّاَئِمُوْنَ يُفْطِرُوْنَ

For example, صائمون يأكلون means the fasting people are eating. And طفق الصائمون يأكلون means that the fasting people have started to eat.

:::note

Some other أفعل which can be a فعل الشروع are أخذ and جعل. These are regularly used in classic arabic texts. However, طفق comes in the قرآن.

:::

## Recap

Now, we have completed part one of the أفعال السماعي, which were the أفعال الناقصة.

The كان وأخواتها usually give a tense or timeframe to the اسم and خبر,
The أفعال المقاربة show that the action is about to take place,
The أفعال الرجاء are verbs which show the speaker's hope,
And أَفْعَالُ الشُّرُوْعِ show that the اسم and خبر's link has commenced.
